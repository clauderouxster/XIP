1. Guidelines :

people inhabit the place, represent it, people/comp legally affiliated to it (citizens).

- instigators of actions that involve physical actions :
"Germany signed", Irak invaded", "Russian intervention"

- instigators of political decisions, attitudes or actions. The maker of political communications:
"a directive from Brussels","Malta submitted"

- experiencers of emotional or mental states

- recipient/beneficiary of actions that are not political or military actions directed at the state as a whole and that can only be directed at people."
"aids programme for Uganda", "to play for Germany", "to accuse Nigeria"

- official attributes of a location : literal
"the language of Germany", " the religion of ..", "Sweden's low/flags/constitution/social system"

-attribute or artifact normally associated with people : place-for-people
"Italy is catholic", "costumes of ..."

- economic situations and attributes of a location as a whole = literal
" ... has hight debts", "Britain's economy"

- economic actions can be targeted at the place as a whole = literal
"exports to Kuwait", "boycott..."

- instigators of economic actions = place-for-people
