+hmm_ADJ - adjective (e.g. "kis" - they cannot be used as nouns: *A kist kérem.)
+hmm_ADV - adverb 
+hmm_ADV_PART - "adverbial participle" (határozói igenév) (e.g. állva)
+hmm_ART - article ("a(z)", "egy")
+hmm_AUX - auxiliary ("szabad")
+hmm_CM - comma (",")
+hmm_CONJ - conjunction (kötőszó) (e.g.: "és")
+hmm_DPRON - deictic pronoun (mutató névmás) (e.g.: "ez")
+hmm_FPART - "future participle" (beálló melléknévi igenév) (e.g.: "megírandót")
+hmm_GPRON - "general pronoun" (általános névmás)  
	     (The general pronouns are: "mind(en)", "mindegyik", "mindenik", 
             "bár-", "semmi-", "akár-")
+hmm_IPRON - "indefinite pronoun" (határozatlan névmás) 
	      (The indefinite pronouns are: "miegymás", "más(ik)", 
	      "egyik", "egyéb", "egynémely(ik)", "egyvalami", 
	      "egyvalaki", "némi", "némely(ik)", "valamelyes", "valami-",
	      "holmi")
+hmm_INF - infinitive (e.g.: csinálni)
+hmm_INTERJ - interjection (e.g.: hejehuja)
+hmm_LS - list (e.g.: 1))
+hmm_MEA - mesure (e.g.: kg)
+hmm_NA - noun-adjective: adjectives that can also be used as nouns, e.g.: 
            "A kéket kérem."
+hmm_N - noun
+hmm_NUMBER - number (e.g: 1)
+hmm_NUM - numeral (számnév) (e.g.: kettő)
+hmm_NUMPRON - numeral pronoun (e.g.: "annyi")
+hmm_ORD - ordinal number (e.g.: 1.)
+hmm_PP - past participle (múltidejű határozói igenév) (e.g.: "kiosztott"
             (kártya))
+hmm_PPRON - personal pronoun (személyes névmás) (e.g: "te", "neked")
+hmm_POSTPOS - postposition (névutú) (e.g.: "mellett")
+hmm_PREFIX - prefix (both verbal (igekötő) and nominal) (e.g.: "be", "fark-")
+hmm_PR - present participle (folyamatos melléknévi igenév) 
             (e.g.: szaladó)
+hmm_PRON - pronoun (névmás) (e.g.: "ki")
+hmm_PN - proper noun 
+hmm_PUNCT - punctuation (e.g.: ":")
+hmm_RF - reflexive pronoun (visszaható névmás) ("magamaga", "(ön)maga")
+hmm_RPRON - relative pronoun (vonatkozó névmás) (e.g.: "aki")
+hmm_ROM - Roman number (e.g.: "XV")
+SENT - sentence ending punctuation (e.g.: ".", "?")
+hmm_SUFF - suffix alone (e.g.: "-ban" like in "A "Szentivánéji álom"-ban")
+hmm_TRUNC - truncated word (e.g.: "kutya-")
+hmm_VERB - verb

Notes:
1. All nominal tags (NOUN, NADJ, _PRON, PROPN) are tagged in  three cases: 
   NOM (nominative), ACC (acusative) and CASE (the other cases)
2. I am aware that the tagging of pronouns is not very coherent. I tried to
   copy the tagging of the Dictionary of Hungarian inflections by Elekfi
   László, which is not very coherent itself. I consider merging the 
   pronouns into fewer classes, but it would need some time.
